Our Programs

Best Daycares

ClubHouse Kids Daycare will provide a stimulating and safe environment for children ages three months to ten years. Run by a former elementary school educator (Britney DeQuattro), ClubHouse Kids Daycare will be the fun, affordable alternative daycare with the best quality you can find in town. We seek to stimulate and develop children's problem-solving and reactive thinking skills through staff and child directed activities in our daily agenda. Our curriculum is designed to foster creativity, independence, and a lifelong love of learning while providing a nurturing environment where every child can grow and thrive. We believe in building strong partnerships with families to support each child's unique developmental journey.

Infants

(6 WEEKS - MOBILE)

Our nursery at Clubhouse Kids Early Learning Center was designed to create a warm, loving, and secure environment. Clubhouse Kids Early Learning Center's infant day care provides opportunities for each child to grow physically, socially, and emotionally while also being in a safe and nurturing environment. There is no question that your child deserves the best.

Toddlers

(2 YEARS - 3 YEARS)

Clubhouse Kids Early Learning Center's Toddler day care program provides children 18 months — 3 years with a full, busy day. Children this age need to explore their environment and we offer a safe, warm, and loving place to do just that. This day care program is highly supervised, which allows children to listen, smell, climb, touch and try new things on their own.

Pre School

(3 YEARS - 6 YEARS)

Preschool is a fun and exciting time. It is Clubhouse Kids Early Learning Center's goal to make sure each child reaches their full potential, and have fun doing it through our preschool day care program. We offer a full curriculum which encompasses teacher based activities, free time, promotion of gross motor skills and speech and language development.

YOUNGER TODDLER

(18 MONTHS - 2 YEARS)

Our Early Walker Classroom at Clubhouse Kids Early Learning Center's Early Development Center allows your child to explore in their surroundings, while feeling safe and secure in a nurturing environment. We support the child's growth through our curriculum which includes predictable routines such as interactive play, daily projects, music and dance. We also have a designated area filled with age appropriate toys such as small climbers, tumble mats and tents.

Pre - K

(4 1/2 Years - 6 YEARS)

Clubhouse Kids Early Learning Center's Pre-Kindergarten program is designed to meet the needs of our preschoolers and especially those who have missed the September 1st kindergarten cut-off. The curriculum encompasses academic, social, emotional, physical and intellectual development. Your child will be fully prepared to begin kindergarten and will gain the skills, confidence and knowledge necessary for a successful first year in grade school.

About us

Our Philosophy

Children of all ages need lots of encouragement as they grow and learn. At Clubhouse Kids, your child will continue to grow and learn in the many ways you, as parents, have nurtured them at home. As a child grows, each stage brings about new experiences in their lives to learn from. We feel it is important to promote independence and start encouraging children to do thing for themselves as soon as they are able (ex. Feeding themselves, dressing & undressing, using the potty, etc.)

At Clubhouse Kids, we know every child is unique with each child learning and growing at their own pace. As childcare providers, it is our job to recognize these differences and compliment them with encouraging words, bright smiles and big hugs. We feel it is important to let kids be kids and learn through a variety of rich learning experiences and exploration. When creating art, we allow children to be messy as they explore the process of making art, regardless of the product. We encourage children to figure out social discrepancies by modeling behavior and intervening when necessary.

Certified Tutors

Small Class Size

We maintain low student-to-teacher ratios to ensure each child receives individual attention and personalized learning experiences.

Safety First
